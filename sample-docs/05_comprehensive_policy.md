# Comprehensive Compliance Policy

## Ownership
The policy owner is the Compliance Lead, who is responsible and accountable for policy execution.

## Review Cycle
This policy is reviewed quarterly, with an annual governance sign-off by leadership.

## Incident and Breach Management
Any security incident triggers escalation workflow. Breach cases are triaged, documented, and reported.

## Data Lifecycle
Data retention periods are documented by data class. Archive and delete actions follow approved controls.

## Access Governance
Access is granted by role and reviewed against least privilege. Permission changes require documented approval.
